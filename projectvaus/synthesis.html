Synthesis and Analysis

01.Sources of Stress

While conducting Contextual Inquiries and Directed Storytelling, we asked students to describe past situations in which a task in the house caused stress. What we found were several situations that were commonly experienced amongst students, and which were potential problems our system could address. We took these results and placed them in the infographic above, with the severity of the problem increasing graphed vertically, and the frequency we found the problems amongst our interviewees.
