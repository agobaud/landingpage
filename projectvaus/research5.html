Recap of Research

05.Speed Dating Findings

What Users Liked

Systems with controls in one place

“it would be helpful if it was all in one place, cause right now the controls for everything is everywhere”

Systems that prevent hazardous situations

“better to have a false alarm once or twice a year, to just be safe”

Systems that help conserve energy (or money)

“I found it useful to have my computer tell me how to save energy"

“my bill went up so much, that’s how I found out it was bad.”

What Users Didn't Like

Systems that took care of Communication between Roommates

“for me it doesn’t really apply to me, cause usually I just talk to my roommate when she’s in the room”

“we usually use text message and email for stuff”

Systems that guilt-tripped or nagged users

“it would be twice as bad if they saw it and they can’t do anything about it"

“in this case, guilt tripping won’t do anything....unless it bothered the other person...since I only experience the consequences.”

Systems that facilitated sharing

“I haven’t shared food so far”

“We have a communal shelf, but don’t often share the other ones”
