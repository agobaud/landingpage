Recap of Research

04.Speed Dating

Speed Dating Plan

We wanted to recruit participants of both sexes living in on and off campus apartments and houses to go through concepts with both individuals and small groups of up to 4 people.

We had seven possible scenarios developed out of our research that we presented to potential users to gain their feedback on the ideas.

Forgetting to buy certain items at the store
Buying unnecessary supplies
Roommate conflicts concerning household tasks
Forgetting a time-sensitive task such as laundry or dishes
Forgetting essential personal items as user leaves the house
Leaving oven/stove on too long
Forgetting to lock the door/turning appliances off once user leaves the house

BID 2011. School of Design. Carnegie Mellon University
