01.What is VAUS?

VAUS (Virtual Assistant for University Students) is a system designed to help university students live a less stressful life. Students are busy with academics, extracurricular activities, family obligations, and their social lives. At times, keeping up with all of these commitments can be difficult, but VAUS helps the student by helping them with their household responsibilities.

Although VAUS will never take responsibility away from the user, VAUS helps the user get their tasks done in a more efficient manner, and helps to prevent dangerous situations from negligence.
