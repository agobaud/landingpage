Design

01.Design Opportunity

Adapt to Target Audience

Our research findings brought up concerns and pracices of university studends differently than initially considered. These included work routines, roommate interactions and chore/task management

Revise Maintenance Agent

Our research findings indicated the maintenance agent would need to be adjusted to fully address user's needs, specifically being location-neutral and user independent or dependent based on the severity of the scenario.

We Narrowed our Design Focus

The refined focus both allows users to easily monitor items in the house and prevents dangerous situations. This allows users to focus on more desirable activities including schoolwork and social engagements.
